Waldorf Salad

Description

This Waldorf salad recipe is delicious, and you can vary the ingredients to your preference. Try adding diced, roasted chicken to make this salad a meal!

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • White Sugar
  • lemon juice
  • salt
  • apples
  • Celery
  • walnuts
  • raisins

Steps

  1. Whisk together mayonnaise, sufar, lemon juice, and salt in a serving bowl
  2. Stir in apples , celery , walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chillin the rerigerator until ready to serve.
